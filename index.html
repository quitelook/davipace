About Us



Who We Are


Through his vision, hard work and business relationships, our beloved Founder began Davipace Inc. in 2017. We are a Construction Material Distribution Specialist with a heavy focus on Residential and Commercial Concrete. Over the years we have become well known for providing exceptional customer service and quality construction materials to our customers. Our Team of Associates have more than 7 decades of experience in the industry and work closely with Site Contractors and Company Owners to ensure their project construction material needs are always the priority. Davipace is a family business based on honest family values and strives to remain a company we would be proud of.



Our Mission


To provide excellent customer service and offer competitive pricing. We are backed by 70 plus years of knowledge in the Industry! We believe you are not just a customer. You are a Business Partner.
